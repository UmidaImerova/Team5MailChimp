ADOPT AN ORCA

Orca are under threat in the wild from fishing nets, plastic and chemical pollution and a reduction in food stocks due to overfishing. The practice of taking individuals from the wild to be kept in cramped tanks and exploited for entertainment is also a problem.

We are opposed to the keeping of orca in captivity, and work with like-minded organisations to raise awareness of the physical and mental suffering these conditions cause.
